About Ourselves

        V.W.S. is an English medium Public school based on CBSE Pattern with co-education. The school family prepares the students for competitive exams and will give a better future and a sound citizen of our country.

School Aims and Objective School Aims

        V.W.S. aims to be an outstanding school where students enjoy learning, achieve highly and enter in to the adult world with confidence.. We aim to produce an environment that encourages each pupil to develop qualities of self discipline, organisation, motivation and the enjoyment of reading and working hard that makes them confident and competent members of society. We will continue to encourage a strong and effective partnership with parents and the local community. We will continue to develop the literacy, creativity, and communication skills so necessary for pupils to access the curriculum and take a full part of society. We will take every opportunity to reward pupils and enhance their self-esteem whilst supporting one another in demanding the highest academic and social standards. We will offer a broad and balanced curriculum which eveloves to reflect the needs of pupils and the demands of modern society.
