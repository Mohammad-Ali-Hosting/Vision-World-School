TRANSPORT FACILITIES
            All transport charges must be paid in advance by the 15th of every month for which payment is due. Transport facilities available all routes around 15 km from school. If for any reason whatsoever, the school decides to cancel or charge any route of the school bus, the parents/guardians shall have no objections to it and will make their own arrangement of conveyance for their children. If a parent wants to take his child with him during the school hours, he must inform the concerned bus staff or the bus in charge about it.
